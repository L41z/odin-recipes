Simple Pudding

This creamy Brazilian-style flan is a classic dessert made with just 5 ingredients. Perfect for any occasion!



Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 1 hr

Chill Time: 4 hrs

Total Time: 5 hrs 10 mins

Servings: 8


Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz/395g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups whole milk (use the empty condensed milk can to measure)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup white sugar (for caramel)

Directions


Step 1

Make the caramel: Melt sugar in a saucepan over medium heat until golden. Pour into a round mold, swirling to coat the bottom.


Step 2

Blend ingredients: Combine condensed milk, milk, eggs, and vanilla in a blender. Mix until smooth.


Step 3

Bake: Pour mixture into the caramel-coated mold. Place in a water bath and bake at 350°F (180°C) for 50-60 minutes.


Step 4

Chill: Cool completely, then refrigerate for 4+ hours. Invert onto a plate to serve with caramel on top.
